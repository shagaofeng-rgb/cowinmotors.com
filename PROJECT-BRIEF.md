# Cowinmotor Website Project Brief

## Company

Company Name: Quzhou Qiying Import & Export Co., Ltd  
Chinese Name: 衢州市企赢进出口有限公司  
Brand Name: Cowinmotor  
Industry: Automotive parts / auto modification products  
Primary Products: Car Exhaust System, Car Headlights, Car Body Kits  
Primary Markets: United States, Canada, Germany, United Kingdom, Italy, France, Australia  
Primary Customers: Importers, distributors, auto repair shops, car modification shops, performance tuning stores, wholesale buyers  

## Positioning

Build Cowinmotor as a professional automotive parts supplier and one-stop auto modification solution partner for global buyers.

Use professional B2B English. Avoid exaggerated claims. Do not invent certifications, factory ownership, customer names, or official partnerships unless source material is provided.

## Visual Style

Primary Colors: Black + Industrial Yellow  
Secondary Colors: Dark Gray, White, Light Gray  
Style: Industrial, professional, heavy-duty, clean, reliable, technical  
Animation: Simple hover effects, subtle product image zoom, smooth section transitions
