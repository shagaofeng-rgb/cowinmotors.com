# Website Optimization Notes from DOCX

Source file: Designing a Minimal Global English Website for Automotive Headlights, Exhaust Pipes, and Body Kits.docx

## Key Direction

- Use an extremely minimal, premium technical visual style.
- Avoid a flashy factory-story site and avoid a giant marketplace clone.
- Lead with vehicle fitment: year, make, model, trim, body type, region spec, and LHD/RHD where relevant.
- Use a hybrid B2B/B2C commerce model:
  - Direct purchase for stocked standard items.
  - Request Quote for bulky, custom, painted, body-kit, wholesale, or OEM/ODM orders.
- Be transparent that the business is a trading/export company, not necessarily a factory.
- Emphasize sourcing, QC inspection, export support, sample orders, packaging, lead time, shipping, returns, and after-sales.
